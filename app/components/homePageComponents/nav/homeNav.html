An Introduction

TouchBase is the future
A fully integrated system which allows remote access to your home from wherever you are in the world. TouchBase provides the opportunity to control your domain in a way never seen before. From third party access to security measures it will change the way you think about your home and what it can do for you. With a focus on reliability and partnerships with many leading insurance companies you can be sure that TouchBase is the next step in bringing the home of the future into today.

It's features include:

Third party Access - The ability to allow tradespeople or visitors to access your house while you are at work or on the road.

Heating Control - Adjust the air conditioning and room temperatures of your home before you even step foot in the door.

Appliance Control - From kettles to cookers, rice cookers to rotisserie, TouchBase will allow you to prepare your meal from wherever you are.

Security Control - Integrated into the system is fully controllable security features, allowing you to check your property remotely and signal for help when needed.
